About Me

I am Usopp, the brave sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates! My real name is Sogeking, but you can call me Usopp. I come from the tiny village of Syrup on the Gecko Islands, and I've always been a dreamer. My dream? To become a brave warrior of the sea and achieve the title of "Sniper King."

You might hear stories about my tall tales and exaggerations, but that's just me adding a bit of flair to make things interesting. Deep down, I'm a loyal and courageous member of the crew. I joined the Straw Hat Pirates to fulfill my dream and to protect my friends. My sharpshooting skills have come in handy many times, whether it's taking down enemies from a distance or providing support during our adventures.
